What's under the hood

Familiar sounds. Refreshing interface.

14
Wave Shapes
Three oscillator selectors, each with 14 sampled shapes from a Curtis-filtered vintage analog synth — plus per-osc pitch & pan.
30
Impulse Responses
Convolution cross-synthesis of our favorite outboard reverbs and eurorack effects — 15 reverbs + 15 FX, built in.
↻
Stepped Sequencer
Unlike a normal step-sequencer, it only steps when a note arrives — swapping the wave shape per note to bring melodies and chords alive.
∿
Cut-Off LFO & Mod Wheel
A dedicated filter LFO with plenty of shapes, plus a Mod-Wheel control center that maps many parameters to your wheel at once.
≈
Vibrato & Misc
Classic vibrato with speed and fade-in, plus a set of miscellaneous controls to keep shaping and growing your sound.
83
Handcrafted Presets
Leads, basses, pads, keys, brass, winds and hits/FX — no filler, and every one performable straight from the mod wheel.
